Background Information

Learn more about our vision, mission, and objectives.

Vision

To create a poverty-free community where every individual has the opportunity, skills, and resources to live a dignified and fulfilling life.

Mission

To reduce poverty by empowering vulnerable individuals and families through skills development, income-generating opportunities, and community-based support systems that foster long-term economic independence and social inclusion.

Objectives of the Project

  1. Improve livelihoods by providing vocational and entrepreneurial training to low-income individuals.
  2. Promote financial independence through support for small business startups and community savings programs.
  3. Raise awareness about the causes and effects of poverty, encouraging community-driven solutions.
  4. Inspire hope and resilience among people facing economic hardship, helping them turn their struggles into strength.
